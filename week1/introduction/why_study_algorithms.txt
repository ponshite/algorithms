Why Study Algorithms?


What is an algorithm anyway?
Set of well defined rules or a recipe to solve a computational problem.
 -> Shortest path
 -> Number of tasks needed to be completed by a deadline.

Why study algorithms?
Important for all the other branches of computer science.
  * Networking
  * Graphics
  * Cryptography
  * Biology
  ...

Plays a key role in modern technological innovation.
Provides novel "lens" on process outside of computer science and techology.
 -> Evolution, financial markets, etc

Challenging!
Fun!
